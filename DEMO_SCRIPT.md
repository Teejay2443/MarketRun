# MarketRun — Demo Video Script (4 Minutes)

**Tagline:** *Someone is already at the market. Let them help you shop.*

---

## SCENE 1 — Hook & Problem (0:00 – 0:25)

**[VISUAL:** Open on busy Nigerian market footage or stylized motion graphic showing chaotic market scene — crowded aisles, heavy bags, traffic. Transition to a person exhausted on a couch.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Every weekend, millions of Lagosians wake up at dawn to fight traffic, spend three hours navigating crowded market aisles, and haul heavy bags back through public transport. Delivery apps charge two to five thousand naira per trip — and they don't even serve informal markets. But here's the thing: your neighbors are already going to the same market every single day. They have space. They have time. They just need coordination.

**[VISUAL:** Split screen — one side shows the struggle, other side shows a phone with the MarketRun app. Text overlay: "Community-powered peer-to-peer shopping."]

## SCENE 2 — Introducing MarketRun (0:25 – 0:50)

**[VISUAL:** MarketRun landing page loads on screen. Show the hero section with animated headline "Someone is already at the market. Let them help you shop." Scroll through the stats — 12 active shoppers, 234 errands completed, ₦2.1M saved.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> This is MarketRun — a community-powered platform that connects people who need groceries with neighbors already heading to the market. No middlemen. No delivery markup. Just neighbors helping neighbors. Built for API Conference Lagos 2026, powered by Monnify.

**[VISUAL:** Click "Post an Errand" button. Sign-up modal appears. Quick montage of creating an account.]

**[VISUAL:** Logged in as Adebayo (Requester). Navigate to the 4-step errand creation wizard.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Let me show you how it works from the requester's side. I'm Adebayo, and I need fresh tomatoes and peppers for weekend cooking. I'll post an errand.

**[VISUAL — Step 1: Details]**
Fill in:
- Title: "Fresh tomatoes and peppers for weekend cooking"
- Description: "Need ripe tomatoes, scotch bonnets, and onions for jollof rice"
- Market: Select "Balogun Market"

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Step one — I describe what I need and pick my market. We support all major Lagos markets — Balogun, Mile 12, Oshodi, and more.

**[VISUAL — Step 2: Items with AI Suggestions]**
Click "AI Suggest" button. AI suggestions panel appears with items like Tomatoes (2kg, ~₦3,500), Scotch Bonnets (1kg, ~₦2,000), Onions (3 pieces, ~₦1,200). Click "Add All Suggestions." Items populate the shopping list. Show the running total updating.

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Step two — here's where it gets smart. Our AI understands Nigerian markets. Watch this — I click "AI Suggest" and it instantly suggests relevant items with current market prices. Tomatoes, scotch bonnets, onions — all with estimated budgets. One click to add them all. The total updates in real time.

**[VISUAL — Step 3: Location]**
Fill in delivery address and estate name.

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Step three — I enter my delivery address and estate so nearby shoppers can find me.

**[VISUAL — Step 4: Payment]**
Set shopper reward to ₦1,500. Show the order summary with item total + reward. Click "Fund & Post Errand." Redirect to Monnify checkout page. Complete payment simulation. Success page with confetti animation.

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Step four — I set a reward for my shopper and fund the errand. The total — items plus reward — goes through Monnify's secure checkout. Your money is held in escrow. It's only released when you confirm delivery. Safe, transparent, trustless.

## SCENE 4 — The Shopper Flow: Accepting & Shopping (1:40 – 2:20)

**[VISUAL:** Switch to Kemi's account (Shopper). Open the Dashboard. Navigate to "Find Errands" tab. Show list of open errands with search and market filter.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Now let's see the shopper's side. I'm Kemi, and I'm heading to Balogun Market this weekend. I open MarketRun, go to Find Errands, and filter by market. There it is — Adebayo's errand, with a ₦1,500 reward.

**[VISUAL:** Kemi clicks on the errand, views details, clicks "Accept." Status changes from OPEN to ACCEPTED. Show the status timeline updating.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> I review the items, the budget looks fair, and I accept. The errand status updates to ACCEPTED. The money is already in escrow — I know I'll get paid.

**[VISUAL:** Kemi clicks "Start Shopping." Status changes to SHOPPING. Open the real-time chat. Send a message: "I'm at the market now. Tomatoes are ₦4,000 for 2kg — slightly above budget. Want me to go ahead?" Adebayo replies: "That's fine, go ahead!" Show back-and-forth messaging.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> I start shopping and the real-time messaging kicks in. I find the tomatoes are slightly above budget — I message Adebayo, he approves, and I keep going. This is the kind of communication that makes community shopping work.

**[VISUAL:** Kemi clicks "Mark as Delivered." Status changes to DELIVERED. Switch back to Adebayo's view.]

---

## SCENE 5 — Confirmation, Payment Split & Review (2:20 – 2:55)

**[VISUAL:** Adebayo sees the delivered errand. Opens it. Clicks "Confirm Delivery." Monnify transaction split visualization appears — 90% to Kemi (₦4,500), 10% platform fee (₦500). Success confetti.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Adebayo confirms delivery. Monnify automatically splits the payment — ninety percent to the shopper, ten percent platform fee. No manual intervention. Kemi gets paid instantly. And Adebayo leaves a five-star review.

**[VISUAL:** Adebayo opens the review modal, selects 5 stars, writes "Kemi was amazing! Fresh items, delivered on time." Submits. Show Kemi's profile now showing the new rating.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> The review system builds community trust. Every shopper is rated and reviewed, so you always know who you're working with.

## SCENE 6 — AI Chat Assistant (2:55 – 3:15)

**[VISUAL:** Open the AI Chat from the sidebar. Type: "I need items for a birthday party for 20 people." AI responds with a full shopping list — rice, chicken, salad ingredients, drinks, cake supplies — with quantities and prices. Select items directly from chat.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Our AI chat goes beyond suggestions. Tell it the occasion — a birthday party, weekend cooking, baby items — and it builds a complete shopping list with quantities and prices tailored to your market. Select items right from the chat and they flow into your errand.

**[VISUAL:** Navigate to the "Payment Infrastructure" tab in the dashboard. Show each feature one by one.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**

**[VISUAL — Reserved Account]**

**NARRATOR (V/O):**

**[VISUAL — KYC Verification]**

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Shoppers verify their identity with BVN through Monnify's KYC API before they can accept errands. Trust is built in.

**NARRATOR (V/O):**

**[VISUAL — Transaction Splitting & Audit Trail]**
Show the 90/10 split calculation. Scroll through the audit trail with timestamped events.

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Transaction splitting happens at the payment level — automatic, no manual work. And every single event is logged in our audit trail for full transparency.
