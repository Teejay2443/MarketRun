# MarketRun — Demo Video Script (3 Minutes)

**Tagline:** *Someone is already at the market. Let them help you shop.*

---

## SCENE 1 — Hook & Problem (0:00 – 0:20)

**[VISUAL:** Quick cuts — crowded Lagos market, traffic, exhausted person on couch. Then: phone with MarketRun app. Text overlay: "Community-powered peer-to-peer shopping."]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Every weekend, millions of Lagosians fight traffic to reach markets that delivery apps don't even serve. But your neighbors are already going. They have space, they have time. MarketRun connects you.

---

## SCENE 2 — Create Errand + AI + Monnify Checkout (0:20 – 1:10)

**[VISUAL:** Logged in as Adebayo. Dashboard loads.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> I'm Adebayo. I need fresh tomatoes for weekend cooking.

**[VISUAL:** Click "Post an Errand." Fill Step 1 — title, description, select Balogun Market.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> I describe what I need and pick my market.

**[VISUAL:** Step 2 — click "AI Suggest." Items appear: Tomatoes ₦3,500, Scotch Bonnets ₦2,000, Onions ₦1,200. Click "Add All." Total updates.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Our AI understands Nigerian markets — occasion, season, pricing. One click to add everything.

**[VISUAL:** Step 3 — delivery address. Step 4 — set reward ₦1,500. Click "Fund & Post." Monnify checkout loads. Complete payment. Confetti success.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> I set the reward, fund through Monnify. Money is held in escrow — released only when I confirm delivery.

---

## SCENE 3 — Shopper Flow + Real-Time Chat (1:10 – 1:50)

**[VISUAL:** Switch to Kemi's account. Dashboard. "Find Errands" tab. Filter by Balogun. Adebayo's errand appears with ₦1,500 reward.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> I'm Kemi. I'm heading to Balogun. I find Adebayo's errand — reward looks good. I accept.

**[VISUAL:** Click "Accept." Status: OPEN → ACCEPTED. Click "Start Shopping." Status: SHOPPING. Open chat.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> I start shopping. Tomatoes are slightly over budget — I message Adebayo.

**[VISUAL:** Chat: "Tomatoes are ₦4,000 for 2kg — slightly above budget. Want me to go ahead?" Reply: "That's fine, go ahead!" Kemi delivers.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> He approves. I deliver. He confirms. Monnify auto-splits — ninety percent to me, ten percent platform fee.

**[VISUAL:** Adebayo clicks "Confirm Delivery." Split animation: ₦4,500 to Kemi, ₦500 platform. Adebayo leaves 5-star review.]

---

## SCENE 4 — AI Chat (1:50 – 2:10)

**[VISUAL:** Open AI Chat. Type: "I need items for a baby naming ceremony for 30 people." AI responds with full list — rice, chicken, drinks, cake supplies — with quantities and prices.]

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
> Our AI chat builds complete shopping lists from occasions. Nigerian context — naming ceremonies, owambe, weekend cooking. Select items directly into your errand.

**NARRATOR (V/O):**
